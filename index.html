C-A-L Pickup is FREE and available 7 days a week!

C-A-L Pickup notifications will arrive via email. Most orders will be ready within 24-hours, however, please allow for extra processing time on nights, weekends, and holidays. Some Restrictions apply.

Curbside:

  • Park in one of the designated C-A-L Pickup parking spaces and call the number listed on the sign.
  • Have your ID and order number handy.
  • A Team Member will request access to your trunk, back seat, or direct you to a loading area (for large/oversized items) then they'll load your order.

In-Store:

  • Once you're in the store, locate the C-A-L Pickup in-store area for assistance from one of our Team Members.
  • Have you ID and order number handy.

Most orders will be ready within 24-hours however if your order was placed after store hours, on a weekend, or a holiday it may take longer. C-A-L Pickup is available 7 days a week, during the same hours as your local store hours.
